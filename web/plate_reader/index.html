Help & Assumptions

The parser searches for a header containing standard 96-well names and paired time columns with a T suffix. Blank subtraction uses wells marked role=blank. Vehicle/control normalization uses wells marked role=vehicle or role=control. Statistics are intentionally simple: Welch t-test for two groups, one-way ANOVA for more groups.
